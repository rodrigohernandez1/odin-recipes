Carbonara

Description

Pasta carbonara is a classic Italian dish made with simple ingredients: pasta (usually spaghetti), eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, pancetta or guanciale (Italian cured pork), and black pepper. The key to a good carbonara is to create a creamy sauce without using cream; the creaminess comes from the eggs and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 200g of spaghetti
  • 100g of pancetta
  • 50g of Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Prepare the Pancetta
  3. Cook the Pancetta
  4. Prepare the Egg Mixture
  5. Combine Pasta and Pancetta
  6. Make the Carbonara Sauce
  7. Serve
