Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients. They are incredibly versatile and can be made with different types of fillings, ranging from meats to vegetables, making them a beloved dish worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortilla
  • Meat, (al pastor is the best)
  • Salsa
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Preparing the meat
  2. Warming the tortillas
  3. Assembling the taco
  4. Serving
